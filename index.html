WHAT IS JETX?
JETX IS A GAME FROM SMARTSOFT WHERE YOU CAN QUICKLY AND EASILY WIN MONEY.
HOW TO PLAY JETX?
PLACE A BET BEFORE THE JET TAKES OFF. YOU CAN PLACE A SINGLE BET OR TWO BETS ON THE SAME JETX FLIGHT. THE MINIMUM STAKE IS 0.5 ETB, AND THE MAXIMUM IS 20000 ETB. USE THE +/- BUTTONS ALONGSIDE EACH BET OPTION TO ADJUST THE BET SIZE.
USE THE COLLECT BUTTON TO COLLECT WINNINGS. TRY TO FLY AS HIGH AS POSSIBLE AND CASH OUT BEFORE THE JET EXPLODES. FLIGHT HEIGHT IS NOT DETERMINED AND VARIES FROM 1 TO THE MAX WIN CAP.
USE AUTOPLAY TO AUTOMATICALLY PLACE THE SAME BET VALUE FOR EACH NEW ROUND. YOU CAN DO THIS ON ONE OR BOTH BET SELECTIONS. THE MINIMUM ODDS FOR AUTO COLLECT IS 1.35X.
IN THE LEFT CORNER OF THE GAME PANEL, COEFFICIENTS STATISTICS AND GAME HISTORY ARE PRESENTED. VALUES ARE DISPLAYED IN GREEN AND RED: THE RED COLOUR INDICATES THE COEFFICIENTS THAT ARE LESS THAN 1.5 AND GREEN MORE THAN 1.5.
HOW IS THE WIN CALCULATED?
THE POTENTIAL PROFIT IS CALCULATED BY MULTIPLYING THE CURRENT FLIGHT COEFFICIENT AND THE MADE BET.
MINIMUM MULTIPLIER (COEFFICIENT) IS 1.00X. MAX WIN PER BET IS 1000000ETB.
WHEN THE MAXIMUM WIN CAP IS REACHED, THE WIN AMOUNT IS AUTO COLLECTED. THE SPEED OR SKILL OF THE PLAYER DOES NOT INFLUENCE, OR INCREASE THE CHANCES OF WINNING, OR DEFINE THE OUTCOME OF THE GAME.
HOW DO I GET THE WINNINGS?
USE THE COLLECT BUTTON BEFORE THE EXPLOSION (THE PROFIT IS INSTANTLY REFLECTED ON THE BALANCE). IF THE EXPLOSION HAPPENS, THE STAKE IS CONSIDERED LOST.
RTP FOR THE GAME IS 96.7-98.8%.
THE MAXIMUM RTP CAN BE ACHIEVED BY THE PLAYER WHEN OPTING TO BET ON THE LOWEST POSSIBLE CRASH MULTIPLIER (COEFFICIENT) ALSO WHEN MAXIMUM COEFFICIENT TO 25000X IS REACHED, THE WIN AMOUNT IS AUTO COLLECTED. JETX IS A REALTIME GAME, FOR WHICH REASON ACTIONS WITH CAHSOUT BUTTONIS SUBJECT TO DELAYS DEPENDING ON YOU INTERNET CONNECTION, USUALLY AROUND 200-300 MS. WHEN YOU CLICK CASHOUT YOU TRIGGER A PAYOUT REQUEST, WHICH IS FURTHER SETTLED AT THE VALUE OF AN ACTUAL MULTIPLIER BY THE TIME IT IS PROCESSED BY GAME SERVER, PROVIDING THERE IS NO CRASH.
WHAT HAPPENS IF I GET DISCONNECTED?
IF THE PLAYER GETS DISCONNECTED AND THE BET IS ACTIVE: 1. THE BET CONTINUES TO PLAY UNTIL THE ROUND FINISHES, THE PLAYER CAN MANUALLY COLLECT THE WIN IF RETURNING BEFORE THE ROUND END. OTHERWISE, THE BET CONTINUES TO PLAY UNTIL IT LOSES OR REACHES THE MAX WIN CAP. 2. IF THE AUTOCASHOUT IS ENABLED, THE WIN WILL AUTOMATICALLY BE COLLECTED IF THE JET REACHES THE PRE-SETTED MULTIPLIER. THE WINNING AMOUNT WILL BE ADDED TO YOUR BALANCE.
HOW ARE THE JACKPOTS PLAYED OUT?
IF JACKPOT IS ENABLED, ONLY ONE PLAYER SELECTED BY THE PROGRAM WINS THE JACKPOT.
THE USER'S BET MUST BE AT LEAST 1 ETB, AND THE MINIMUM COEFFICIENT DURING THE WITHDRAWAL OF THE BET AT LEAST 1.4. CONTRIBUTION TO THE JACKPOT IS 0.21%, IRRESPECTIVE OF WHETHER THE BET QUALIFIES FOR WINNING THE JACKPOT OR NOT. THE HIGHEST RTP OF 97.9% CAN BE ACHIEVED WHEN PLAYING BETS THAT QUALIFY FOR JACKPOTS BY BETTING ON THE COEFFICIENT 2.00.
IF A PLAYER HITS THE JACKPOT, ONLY 70% OF THE JACKPOT AMOUNT IS CREDITED TO THE PLAYER. THE REMAINING 30% OF THE JACKPOT IS CARRIED OVER TO FUND THE NEXT JACKPOT.
